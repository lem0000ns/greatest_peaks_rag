					Madam Marsh
			
Gender Female Dates fl. 1990s
Species / Race Witch
First Introduced PA3: The Knight Bus

Madam Marsh was a witch who repeatedly travelled on the Knight Bus, despite suffering from motion sickness.
Madam Marsh is, according to Rowling, simply an "extra" with no further part to play in the saga (JKR).
