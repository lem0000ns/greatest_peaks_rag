Comet 180
The Comet 180 was the second of the Comet series of racing brooms. This model was released by the Comet Trading Company in 1938 (QA9).
The One Eighty appeared the year following the release of the Cleansweep Three broom, as the competition between the Comet and Cleansweep broom companies continued to escalate (QA9).
