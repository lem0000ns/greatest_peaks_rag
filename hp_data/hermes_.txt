					Hermes
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1990s
Species / Race Screech owl
Profession Post owl
First Introduced CS3: The Burrow

Hermes was a screech owl belonging to Percy Weasley (PA5). Molly and Arthur bought Hermes for Percy when he was made Prefect (CS3).
Hermes was an ancient Greek god who, among other things, acted as a messenger (source: Encyclopaedia Britannica).
