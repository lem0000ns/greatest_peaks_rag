					Tom
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1930s - 1990s
Species / Race Wizard Hair Bald Distinguishing Features toothless
Profession Barman
First Introduced PS5: Diagon Alley

Tom was the bald, toothless, gloomy old innkeeper of the Leaky Cauldron (PS5, PA3, PA4). He was succeeded by Hannah Abbott (OBT/CH).
Tom was already working at the Leaky Cauldron when Dumbledore told Tom Riddle about Hogwarts (HBP13).
