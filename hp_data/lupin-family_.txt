					Lupin family
			
Species / Race Wizard
First Introduced PA18: Moony, Wormtail, Padfoot and Prongs

A Wizarding family blighted by Lycanthropy when young Remus Lupin was bitten by the werewolf, Fenrir Greyback.
Members include:
Lyall Lupin, father of Remus Lupin
Hope Howell Lupin, Muggle mother of Remus Lupin
Remus Lupin, Werewolf and Hogwarts Professor
Teddy Lupin, son of Remus and his wife, Nymphadora Tonks
Last name meaning: Lupus='wolf' in Latin.
