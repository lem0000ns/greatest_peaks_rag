First Introduced BFT: The Black Family Tree

Ursula Flint was the wife of Hogwarts Headmaster Phineas Nigellus Black, with whom she had five children (BFT).
Ursula had five children with Phineas Nigellus Black between 1877 and 1889: Sirius, Phineas (who was disowned for supporting muggle rights), Cygnus, Belvina, and Arcturus.
"Ursula" from "Ursa," the constellations Ursa Major and Ursa Minor (big bear and little bear), in keeping with the Black Family having members named after stars and constellations.
