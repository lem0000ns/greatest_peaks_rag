“No, no, it’s not the toilet, it’s the Potter boy’s hearing — they’ve changed the time and venue — it starts at eight o’clock now and it’s down in old Courtroom Ten —”
-- Perkins (OP7)
A “stooped, timid-looking old wizard with fluffy white hair” who works with Arthur Weasley at the Ministry (CS4). Perkins lent a couple of tents to Arthur for the Quidditch World Cup. Perkins doesn’t camp much any more because of his lumbago (GF7).
When Harry's hearing time and venue were changed, Perkins informed Mr. Weasley on time (OP7). He also attends Bill and Fleur's wedding (DH8).
