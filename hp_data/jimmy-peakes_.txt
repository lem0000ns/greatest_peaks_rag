Jimmy Peakes was a Gryffindor student from 1994 to 2001, and a Beater on the Gryffindor Quidditch team.
A "short but broad-chested third-year boy;" he is two-thirds Ron's height (HBP14). Plays Quidditch "ferociously." (HBP11)
He gave Harry the scroll from Dumbledore when he had located the locket Horcrux (HBP25).
Though underage, he was one of the people who wanted to stay and fight at the Battle of Hogwarts, but was made to leave by McGonagall (DH31).
He is in the same year and dormitory as Dennis Creevey (HBP11).
