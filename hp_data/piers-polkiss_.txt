					Piers Polkiss
			
Gender Male Birthday born 1980
Blood Status Muggle Distinguishing Features Scrawny, with a face like a rat
First Introduced PS2: The Vanishing Glass

Dudley’s best friend (PS2).
Piers Polkiss helped Dudley beating up others. He attended Smeltings with Dudley (PS3) and as present when Harry let escape the snake at London's Zoo.
Piers calls Dudley Big D (OP1).
As Piers was the one to call to attention what the snake was doing when Harry spoke to it, Piers must have heard Harry speak parseltongue.
