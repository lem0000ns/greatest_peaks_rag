Bigonville Bombers 
The Bigonville Bombers are a European Quidditch team from Bigonville in Luxembourg.
The Bombers are known for their team’s offensive strategies and high scoring (QA8).
Related images:
    
Bigonville, a small town in western Luxembourg, does not have any sports teams (Quidditch or otherwise) mentioned in its Wikipedia entry (Wikipedia).
As Luxembourg is such a small country, the Bombers may possibly provide many of the players for Luxembourg's National Team.
