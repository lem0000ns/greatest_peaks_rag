Theodard Fontaine was one of MACUSA’s original twelve aurors (Pm).
Fontaine and the other eleven original American aurors volunteered knowing that the job would be dangerous and demanding; Fontaine was one of only two who survived to old age (Pm).
The descendants of the original twelve aurors are generally revered in wizarding America. Theodard's direct descendent Agilbert Fontaine is the current Headmaster of Ilvermorny (Pm).
Fontaine is of French origin and denotes a fountain, spring, or well (sources: Behind the Name, Wikipedia).
