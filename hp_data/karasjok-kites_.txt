Karasjok Kites 
A Quidditch team from Norway
The Kites were defeated in the 1956 European Cup finals by the Caerphilly Catapults (QA7).
Karasjok is a village located in Finnmark County, in the far northeast of Norway. There are no sports teams of any kind (including Quidditch) mentioned in its Wikipedia entry (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karasjok).
