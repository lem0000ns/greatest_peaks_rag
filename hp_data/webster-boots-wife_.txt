Webster Boot‘s wife was a young Scottish witch who worked at the Ministry of Magic (Pm).
Her and Webster's marriage resulted in the return of a line of the Boot family to its homeland of Britain, where they remain to this day. Their descendants, who include Terry Boot and Albert Boot, were educated at Hogwarts (Pm).
