Billy Stubbs


					Billy Stubbs
			
Gender Male Species / Race Muggle Blood Status Muggle
First Introduced HBP13: The Secret Riddle

Billy Stubbs was another child at Tom Riddle’s orphanage. Billy Stubbs had chickenpox at the time of Dumbledore’s first meeting with Riddle. According to Mrs. Cole, the day after Tom and Billy had had an argument, Billy’s pet rabbit was found hanging from the rafters (HBP13).
