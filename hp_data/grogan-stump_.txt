Grogan Stump was a popular Minister for Magic who served from 1811 to 1819 (Pm).
Established the Department of Magical Games and Sports (or re-established it -- there are conflicting dates about this). He passed important legislation on creatures and beings. Stump was a big fan of the Tutshill Tornadoes Quidditch Team (Pm)
Enjoyed sports, but also helped creatures and beings.
