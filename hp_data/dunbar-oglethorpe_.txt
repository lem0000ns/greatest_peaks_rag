Dunbar Oglethorpe was chief of Q.U.A.B.B.L.E. (the Quidditch Union for the Administration and Betterment of the British League and its Endeavours) (FW).
Dunbar is a locational Scottish surname from the town of the same name in East Lothian (Gaelic dùn "fort" and barr "summit") (Behind the Name).
Oglethorpe is a Yorkshire locational surname derived from the Old Norse personal name Oddke(ti)ll (odd "point of a weapon", ketill "sacrificial cauldron" and thorp "village, settlement, farm") (Internet Surname Database).
