Prudence


					Prudence
			
Gender Female Dates fl. 1269
Blood Status Unknown
First Introduced QA4: The Arrival of the Golden Snitch

Prudence was the sister of Modesty Rabnott. She lived in Aberdeen (QA4).
In a thirteenth-century letter to Prudence now displayed at the Museum of Quidditch, Modesty tells of getting fined for trying to protect a Snidget (QA4).
