					McClivert Clan
			
First Introduced Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (book)

A legendary clan who lived on the Isle of Drear. The McClivert clan feuded with the MacBoon clan. Dugald McClivert, their chief, was killed by Quintius MacBoon. The McCliverts retailated by turning all of the MacBoons into Quintapeds (FB).
