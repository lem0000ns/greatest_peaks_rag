Bulstrode family



A Wizarding Pure-blood family, listed in the ‘Pure-Blood Directory’ (c. 1930) as one of the ‘Sacred Twenty-Eight’ families (Pm). A notable member of this family is Millicent Bulstrode. She attended Hogwarts at the same time as Harry, Ron and Hermione.
