Urquhart Rackharrow


					Urquhart Rackharrow
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1600s
Species / Race Wizard
First Introduced OP22: St Mungo’s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries

Urquhart Rackharrow invented the Entrail-Expelling Curse in the 1600s. A portrait of him hung in the Dai Llewellyn Ward at St. Mungo’s (OP22).
Urquhart = The name of a Scottish clan that was involved in numerous wars and conflicts in British history (source: Wikipedia).
Rackharrow = This name evokes images of torture; a rack is a torture device (source: Wikipedia) and "harrow" means to distress or disturb in a very painful way (source: Dictionary.com).
