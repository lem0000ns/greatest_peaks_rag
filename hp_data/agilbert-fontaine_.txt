					Agilbert Fontaine
			
Gender Male Species / Race Wizard
Profession Headmaster of Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry
First Introduced Pm: Pottermore

Agilbert Fontaine is the current Headmaster of Ilvermorny School of Witchcraft and Wizardry (Pm).
Agilbert is a direct descendant of Theodard Fontaine, one of MACUSA's original twelve Aurors (Pm).
Fontaine is of French origin and denotes a fountain, spring, or well (sources: Behind the Name, Wikipedia).
