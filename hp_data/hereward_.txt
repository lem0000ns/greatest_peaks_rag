					Hereward
			
Gender Male Dates fl. early Middle Ages
Species / Race Wizard Wand One time possessor of the Elder Wand
First Introduced DH21: The Tale of the Three Brothers (chapter)

Hereward was a mad wizard who locked his own father Godelot in his cellar, stealing the Elder Wand from him and leaving him to die (TBB, DH21). He lived in medieval times (TBB).
