					Hilliard Hobday
			
Gender Male Dates fl.1993
Species / Race wizard
First Introduced DP2: Daily Prophet Issue 2

Hilliard Hobday was the father of Egmont Elvert Hobday, whose birth announcement appears in the Daily Prophet (DP2)
Egmont was born on November 30, 1998 to Hilliard and his wife Violetta Hobday (DP2)
While the date printed on DP2 is 8 February 1999, the timeframe for these events is 1992-1993.
