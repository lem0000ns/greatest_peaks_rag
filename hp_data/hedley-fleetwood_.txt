					Hedley Fleetwood
			
Gender Male Species / Race Wizard Patronus Wooly Mammoth
First Introduced BoS: Book of Spells

Hedley Fleetwood was a wizard whose Patronus took the form of a wooly mammoth. This was notable to Miranda Goshawk, who references him in the Book of Spells, because extinct Patronuses are exceptionally rare. (BoS)
He is skilled in producing an extinct creature as his Patronus, a wooly mammoth.
