Stonewall Stormers 
A Quidditch team from Stonewall in Manitoba, Canada
According to Quidditch Through the Ages, the Stormers are one of the three "most accomplished Quidditch teams in the world" (QA8).
Stonewall, a town in the Manitoba region of Canada, has two ice hockey teams and a baseball team in its Wikipedia entry, but no mention of a Quidditch team (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stonewall,_Manitoba).
