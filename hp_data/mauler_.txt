Species / Race Kneazle
Family Groups
		
Scamander family


First Introduced Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (book)

Mauler is one of three pet Kneazles belonging to Newt Scamander and his wife Porpentina (FB).
Newt and Tina own their Kneazles in Dorset where they are living in retirement (at the time of publication of the most recent edition of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them).
