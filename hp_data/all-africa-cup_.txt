All-Africa Cup
The All-Africa Cup is a tournament to determine the top Quidditch team in Africa. It has been won twice by the Gimbi Giant-Slayers (QA8).
Since 1957, Muggle football has a bi-yearly African tournament called the "Africa Cup of Nations" - officially the "Coupe d'Afrique des Nations" (Wikipedia).
