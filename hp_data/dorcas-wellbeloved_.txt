Dorcas Wellbeloved was the founder of the Society for Distressed Witches (fw).
Dorcas = gazelle (from the Greek δορκας). Also, the name of a woman in the Bible (also called Tabitha) who "was always doing good and helping the poor" (Acts 9:36).
