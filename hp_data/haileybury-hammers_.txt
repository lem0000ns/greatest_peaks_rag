Haileybury Hammers 
A Quidditch team from Haileybury in Ontario, Canada
According to Quidditch Through the Ages, the Hammers are one of the three "most accomplished Quidditch teams in the world" (QA8).
Haileybury is a small town that has been incorporated, with two other nearby towns, into the city of Temiskaming Shores in the Northeastern Ontario region of Canada. There are not any sports teams noted in its Wikipedia listing (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Temiskaming_Shores).
