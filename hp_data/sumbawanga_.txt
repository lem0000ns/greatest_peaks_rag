Sumbawanga
Sumbawanga is a city in Tanzania, Africa
Sumbawanga is home of the Sumbawanga Sunrays, a popular Quidditch team famous for its formation looping (QA8).
Sumbawanga is the capital city of the Rukwa region in Western Tanzania. According to Wikipedia, Sumbawanga means "throw away your witchcraft". (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sumbawanga)
