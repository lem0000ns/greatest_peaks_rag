First Introduced DP2: Daily Prophet Issue 2

Albert Jorkins was a wizard whose daughter Griselda Harmonia Jorkins’s birth was announced in the Daily Prophet (DP2).
Already the parents of Grimwold and Granville, Albert and his wife Primrose Jorkins, had their third child, Griselda, on December 6, 1998 (DP2)
While the date printed on DP2 is 8 February 1999, the timeframe for these events is 1992-1993.
