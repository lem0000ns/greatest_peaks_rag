Josef Wronski was a talented Quidditch player from Poland who played Seeker for the Grodzisk Goblins. He was known for inventing a dangerous Quidditch move called the Wronski Defensive Feint (QA8).
Josef comes from the Latin name Ioseph and Greek Ιωσηφ (Ioseph), both derived from the Hebrew name יוֹסֵף (Yosef), meaning "he will add" (Behind the Name).
Wronski is a nickname derived from the Polish word wrona ("crow") (Behind the Name).
