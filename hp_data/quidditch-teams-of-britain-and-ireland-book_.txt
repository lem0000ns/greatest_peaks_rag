Quidditch Teams of Britain and Ireland (book)
Quidditch Teams of Britain and Ireland is a book with descriptions of the various Quidditch teams in the UK.
Hermione Granger gave a copy of this to Harry Potter as a Christmas present during their fourth year (GF23). It was one of the books Harry tried reading as a distraction while cooped up in Number Four Privet Drive after the Dementors attacked before he left for his fifth year at Hogwarts (OP3).
