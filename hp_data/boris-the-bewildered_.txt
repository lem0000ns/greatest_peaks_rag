					Boris the Bewildered
			
Gender Male Species / Race Statue Distinguishing Features Lost look, gloves on the wrong hands
First Introduced GF23: The Yule Ball

"Tell you what, use the prefects' bathroom. Fourth door to the left of that statue of Boris the Bewildered on the fifth floor.  Password's 'pine fresh.'  Gotta go ..."
-- Cedric Diggory giving Harry a hint about the Second Task (GF23)
Boris the Bewildered is the name of the subject of a statue located on the fifth floor of Hogwarts four doors down from the Prefects’ bathroom (GF23). Boris looks lost and has his gloves on the wrong hands (GF25).
