First Introduced GF8: The Quidditch World Cup

A Bulgarian wizarding family that has produced at least two world-class Quidditch Beaters (Pm).
Vulchanov
Boris Vulchanov
The elder Vulchanov was a Beater on the Bulgarian National Team that lost to Ireland in the 1994 Quidditch World Cup (GF8, Pm).
The elder Vulchanov’s son, Boris, is also a Beater and was a member of the winning Bulgarian team at the 2014 Quidditch World Cup (Pm).
Vulchanov means "volcano", deriving from Bulgarian вулка́н (vulkan) (Dictionary.com). It is similar in spelling to the name of the Bulgarian cinema actor and director Rangel Valchanov (Wikipedia).
They may be related to Nerida Vulchanova, the founder of Durmstrang Institute (Pm).
