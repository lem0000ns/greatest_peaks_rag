Amarillo Lestoat was a flamboyant american vampire. Author of A Vampire’s Monologue – intended to bore the reader into a stupor making him/her easier prey for vampires.
'Lestoat' is a pun on the name 'Lestat,' the main character of Anne Rice's vampire novel Interview with a Vampire. A stoat is a the largest of the weasle family, sometimes called an ermine.
"Amarillo" is the name of a city in Texas = "yellow" in Spanish, for the wildflowers surrounding the town
