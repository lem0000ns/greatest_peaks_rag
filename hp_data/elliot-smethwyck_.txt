					Elliot Smethwyck
			
Gender Male Dates fl. early 1800s
Species / Race Wizard
First Introduced QA9: The Development of the Racing Broom

... the invention of the Cushioning Charm by Elliot Smethwyck in 1820 went a long way towards making broomsticks more comfortable than ever before...
-- from Quidditch Through the Ages (QA9)
Elliot Smethwyck was the inventor, in 1820, of the Cushioning Charm. The spell was used to provide an invisible cushion on the handle of a broom, making broom travel far more comfortable (QA9).
