Tilly Toke


					Tilly Toke
			
Gender Female Birth 1903
Species / Race Witch
First Introduced Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (book)

Recipient of the Order of Merlin First Class as a result of the Ilfracombe dragon attack of 1932. An errant Welsh Green dragon attacked a beach full of sunbathers. Tilly and her family, who were on holiday there, were credited with saving many Muggle lives as well as wiping their memory afterwards. Tilly now appears on a Famous Wizards card (FB, FW).
Tilly Toke must be very adept at Memory Charms, having performed the largest batch of them in the twentieth century (FB).
