Gulliver Pokeby


					Gulliver Pokeby
			
Gender Male Birth 1750
Death 1750
Species / Race Wizard

Gulliver Pokeby was a wizard who studied magical birds. He wrote about his discovery that the cry of an augurey foretells rain, not death, in his book Why I Didn’t Die When the Augurey Cried (FB, Pm).
