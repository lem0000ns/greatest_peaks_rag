B. Dunstan was a Hogwarts student who checked Quidditch Through The Ages out of the Hogwarts library (QA).
Dunstan is a surname of Anglo-Saxon origin. As a given name it derives from dunn "dark, brown" and stan "stone". The surname may also denote someone who originally came from the village of Dunstone in Devonshire (Internet Surname Database).
