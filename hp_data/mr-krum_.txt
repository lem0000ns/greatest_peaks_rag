					Mr. Krum
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1995
First Introduced GF31: The Third Task

Mr. Krum was the father of Bulgarian Quidditch player Viktor Krum (GF31).
Mr. Krum and his wife, Mrs. Krum, attended the third task of the Triwizard Tournament along the with parents of the other Triwizard champions (GF31).
