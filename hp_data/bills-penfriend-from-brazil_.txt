A boy from Castelobruxo, a wizarding school in Brazil, who was penfriends with Bill Weasley (GF7).
He sent Bill a cursed hat that shrivelled up his ears when Bill said he couldn't go on an exchange, because the Weasleys couldn't afford it (GF7).
