Death 1555
Species / Race Witch
Profession Builder and first landlady of the Leaky Cauldron.
First Introduced FW: The Famous Wizard Cards

Daisy Dodderidge was the builder and first landlady of the Leaky Cauldron (WoM). Daisy wanted to create a gateway between the Muggle world and Diagon Alley. Her inn became famous for its welcoming atmosphere (and for the connection to Diagon Alley, of course) (FW).
JKR's Wizard of the Month for March, 2006.
