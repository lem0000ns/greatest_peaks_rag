Inigo Imago was the author of the book The Dream Oracle which is used as a textbook in fifth year Divination class at Hogwarts (OP12).
"imago" = an idealized image of someone, formed in childhood and persisting in later life, from L. imago = image
The name Inigo sounds similar to the word "enigma" which means something, often a person, which is a mystery or a puzzle.
