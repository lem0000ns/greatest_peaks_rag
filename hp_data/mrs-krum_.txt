Mrs. Krum


					Mrs. Krum
			
Gender Female Dates fl. 1995
First Introduced GF31: The Third Task

Mrs. Krum was the mother of Bulgarian Quidditch player Viktor Krum (GF31).
Mrs. Krum and her husband, Mr. Krum, attended the third task of the Triwizard Tournament along with the families of the other Triwizard champions (GF31).
