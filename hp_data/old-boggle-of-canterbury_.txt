Old Boggle of Canterbury


					Old Boggle of Canterbury
			
Species / Race Boggart
First Introduced Pm: Pottermore

The Old Boggle of Canterbury was a tiny Boggart who lived in a cave near the town of Canterbury. The Boggart tricked the Muggles of the area into believing he was a mad, cannibalistic hermit by using echos (Pm:Boggart).
Tricking Muggles
"boggle" or "bogle" is derived from the Middle-English Bugge (of which the term bogey is also derived) which is in turn a cognate of the German term word bögge (of which böggel-mann ("Goblin") is derived) (Wikipedia)
