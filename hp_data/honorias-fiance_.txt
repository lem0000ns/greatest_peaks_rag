					Honoria's fiance
			
Gender Male Species / Race wizard
First Introduced WHH: The Warlock’s Hairy Heart

A wizard who worked in the Improper Use of Magic Office who was once engaged to Albus Dumbledore’s aunt Honoria.  Honoria called off the engagement because she claimed the wizard “had a hairy heart,” though rumor had it the engagement was broken when the wizard was discovered fondling Horklumps.  Albus Dumbledore finds it very difficult to see why anyone would want to fondle Horklumps.
