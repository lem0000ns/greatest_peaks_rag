Lorcan d’Eath is a very popular part-vampire singer whose song “Necks to You” spent nineteen weeks in the Number 1 slot (JKR, WoM).
Lorcan = "little fierce one"(Irish Gaelic).
d'Eath, without the apostrophe, spells "death"
JKR's Wizard of the Month for November 2006 (JKR).
