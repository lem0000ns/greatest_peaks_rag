					Bungy the Budgie
			
Species / Race budgerigar
First Introduced OP1: Dudley Demented

"And finally, Bungy the budgie has found a novel way of keeping cool this summer..."
-Muggle news anchor (OP1)
Bungy the Budgie was a budgerigar, or budgie, that could water-ski. He resided at the Five Feathers in Barnsley (OP1).
Mary Dorkins reported on Bungy and his talent for the Muggle news in the summer of 1995 (OP1).
Forbidden by the Dursleys from watching the news, Harry Potter listened to the news coverage on Bungy while lying in a flower bed below an open window (OP1).
