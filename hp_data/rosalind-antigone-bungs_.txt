Rosalind Antigone Bungs was an individual whose name Hermione Granger encountered in a Hogwarts library book while researching who R.A.B. might be (HBP30).
"Rosalind" and "Antigone" are both names of characters in famous plays (Shakespeare's As You Like It and Sophocles's Theban plays, respectively).
