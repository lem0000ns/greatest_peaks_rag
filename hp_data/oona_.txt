Oona


					Oona
			
Gender Female Dates fl. 1100s
Species / Race Witch
First Introduced QA3: The Game From Queerditch Marsh

Oona was an innkeeper in Yorkshire in the 1100s. She provided the local “Kwidditch” team with the barrels they used for goals and afterwards, gave free mead all night to celebrate the team’s victory over Ilkley (QA3).
