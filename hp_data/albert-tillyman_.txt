Albert Tillyman


					Albert Tillyman
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1855
First Introduced Pm: Pottermore

Albert Tillyman was a wizard and a bully whose wife, Violet, went missing after they argued. He believed she had disappeared in the Floo Network, which caused a national scare. Unbeknownst to him, she had traveled to the wrong house and left him for the man she met there, Myron Otherhaus (Pm).
Spouse: Violet Tillyman, whom he argued with often, and who went missing.
