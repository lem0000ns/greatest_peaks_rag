Martha Sayre Steward‘s husband was a No-Maj from the Pocomtuc tribe (Pm).
Martha, who was a Squib, met her husband through being friends with his sister. Painful as it was to live in a world she could never fully be part of, she decided to live with her husband as a No-Maj (Pm).
