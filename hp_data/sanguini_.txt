Sanguini


					Sanguini
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1996
Species / Race Vampire
First Introduced HBP15: The Unbreakable Vow

Sanguini was a vampire and a friend of Eldred Worple, with whom he attended Professor Slughorn’s Christmas Party. During the party, Worple had to keep an eye on Sanguini just to be sure he did not try to attack anyone (HBP15).
Sanguini = From the Latin sanguis, meaning "blood" (source: Wikipedia).
