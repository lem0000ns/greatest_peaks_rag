Rutherford Poke


					Rutherford Poke
			
Gender Male Species / Race Wizard
Profession Healer
First Introduced Pm2.4.1

A Healer and spokeswizard for St Mungo’s. In 2010, Poke stated that the Wizarding public needed to stop trying to create imitation Floo Powder and blowing themselves “out of the chimney.”
"Poke" may be a play on the idea that doctors "poke and prod you."
