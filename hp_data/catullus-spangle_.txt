Species / Race Wizard

Spangle was a renowned researcher of Charms in the eighteenth century, notable for his work on Patronuses (and, specifically, the forms which Patronuses take).  The book Charms of Defence and Deterrence is considered his masterwork.
Catullus was a Latin poet in Ancient Rome, whose works are considered shocking given how explicit they are.
