					Ciceron Harkiss
			
Gender Male Species / Race wizard
First Introduced HBP4: Horace Slughorn

Ciceron Harkiss was the wizard who gave Ambrosius Flume (owner of Honeydukes) his first job. Favored student of Horace Slughorn, probably a member of the Slug Club (HBP4).
Ciceron, or Cicerón, is the name for the ancient Roman Cicero is a variety of European languages.
