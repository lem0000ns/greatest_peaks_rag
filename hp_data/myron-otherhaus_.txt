					Myron Otherhaus
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1855
Species / Race Wizard Distinguishing Features Handsome
First Introduced Pm: Pottermore

Myron Otherhaus was a handsome wizard who lived in Bury St Edmunds. He fell in love with Violet Tillyman when she accidentally arrived in his house via the Floo Network (Pm).
Partner: Violet Tillyman
Children: Myron and Violet had seven children together.
