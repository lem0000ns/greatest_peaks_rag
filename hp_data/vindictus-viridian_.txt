					Vindictus Viridian
			
Gender Male Species / Race Wizard
Profession Author
First Introduced PS5: Diagon Alley

Professor Vindictus Viridian was the author of Curses and Countercurses (Bewitch Your Friends and Befuddle Your Enemies with the Latest Revenges: Hair Loss, Jelly-Legs, Tongue- Tying and Much, Much More) (PS5, Pm).
Vindictus = from the Latin vindictaDictionary.com, meaning "revenge" (source: ).
Viridian = Viridian is a shade of green and the name of a blue-green pigment (source: Wikipedia).
When Harry encounters Professor Viridian's book on his first visit to Diagon Alley, it is called Curses and Countercurses (Bewitch Your Friends and Befuddle Your Enemies with the Latest Revenges: Hair Loss, Jelly-Legs, Tongue- Tying and Much, Much More) (PS5). On the old Pottermore site, the titled was shortened to Curses and Countercurses (Pm).
