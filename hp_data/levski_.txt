Levski is a Quidditch player, who was Chaser on the Bulgarian National Team at the 1994 Quidditch World Cup (GF8).
Along with his Chaser colleagues Dimitrov and Ivanova, Levski was a member of the Bulgarian team that lost to Ireland in the 1994 tournament. The other members of the 1994 team were: Viktor Krum (Seeker); Volkov and Vulchanov (Beaters); and Zograf (Keeper) (GF8).
Levski is the father of Bogomil Levski, one of the Beaters on the Bulgarian National Team that won the 2014 Quidditch World Cup (Pm).
Levski means "like a lion" in archaic Bulgarian (Baby Namespedia). The currency of Bulgaria is the lev and a famous Bulgarian hero is Vasil Levski.
A videogame with Famous Wizards cards gives him a first name of "Alexei".
