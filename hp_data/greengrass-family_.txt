					Greengrass family
			

The Greengrass family is a Wizarding Pure-blood family, listed in the ‘Pure-Blood Directory’ (c. 1930) as one of the ‘Sacred Twenty-Eight‘ families (Pm).
Notable members:
Astoria Greengrass
Daphne Greengrass
