Vincent Duc de Trefle-Picques


					Vincent Duc de Trefle-Picques
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1793
Species / Race wizard
School
		
							Beauxbatons - Student
					


Famous European wizard who escaped the Terror (1793-1794) by casting a concealment charm on his neck and feigning decapitation.  He is one of Beauxbatons’s most renowned alumni.
"Trefle" = French for clubs (card suit)
"Picque" = French for spades (card suit)
