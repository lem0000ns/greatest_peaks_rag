Poppy


					Poppy
			
Dates fl 1926
Species / Race Bowtruckle
First Introduced WFT: Fantastic Beast and Where to Find Them (film)

Poppy was a Bowtruckle in Newt Scamander’s case (WFT).
Pickett was afraid that the other Bowtruckles, including Poppy, would be mean to him. Poppy and her fellow Bowtruckles besides Pickett allegedly felt that Newt showed favoritism towards Pickett (WFT).
Poppy was part of a clan, or "branch", or Bowtruckles in a tree in Newt Scamander's case (WFT).
