Tchamba Charmers
The Tchamba Charmers are a Quidditch team from Togo in Africa.
The Charmers are known for their ability to do the difficult “reverse pass” move (QA8).
Tchamba is a city in the Prefecture of the same name in the Centrale Region of Togo (and also the tribe living in the area). There are not any sports teams listed in their Wikipedia entry (Wikipedia).
