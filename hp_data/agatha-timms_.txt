					Agatha Timms
			
Gender Female Species / Race Witch Distinguishing Features Described as "little," which suggests that she's short.
Profession Eel farmer
First Introduced GF7: Bagman and Crouch

Agatha Timms was a short witch who owns an eel farm. At the 1994 Quiddtch World Cup, Agatha bets half shares in her eel farm on a week-long match (GF7).
