					Andorran Minister for Magic
			
Dates fl. 1980s
Species / Race Wizard
Profession Minister of Magic
First Introduced GF28: The Madness of Mr Crouch

The Andorran Minister for Magic was mentioned by “mad” Mr. Crouch in the Forbidden Forest just before his death.
Crouch was having a memory of an earlier time during his career at the Ministry of Magic when he needed to reply to the Andorran Minister for Magic (GF28).
