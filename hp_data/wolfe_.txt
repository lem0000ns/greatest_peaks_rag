					Shikoba Wolfe
			
Gender Female Dates Fl. 1920s
Species / Race Witch Wand carved wood with Thunderbird tail-feather
Profession Wandmaker
First Introduced Pm: Pottermore

Shikoba Wolfe was one of the four great wandmakers in United States in the 1920s. Wolfe was famous for her intricately carved wands with the core of a Thunderbird tailfeather. Her heritage was Choctaw (Pm).
Wandmaking
According to Nameberry, Shikoba means "feather" in Choctaw.
