					Lovegood family
			
Species / Race Wizard
First Introduced GF6: The Portkey

A small, eccentric wizarding family residing near Ottery St. Catchpole.
Notable members of this family include:
Xenophilius Lovegood
Pandora Lovegood
Luna Lovegood
Publishing, an affinity for unknown creatures, and vivid imaginations.
Cf. the Whateley family from H. P. Lovecraft's short story "The Dunwich Horror" (source: "The Dunwich Horror").
