					Elias Grimstone
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1870s
Species / Race Wizard
Profession Broom designer and manufacturer
First Introduced QA9: The Development of the Racing Broom

Elias Grimstone was the developer of the Oakshaft 79 broom (QA9).
The Oakshaft 79, ridden by Jocunda Sykes, was the first to carry a passenger across the Atlantic Ocean (QA9).
grim [Eng] can mean "sad", "worried", "unpleasant" and "serious". Grimstone is also the name of a small village in rural Dorset and there is a Grimstone Manor in Devon.
