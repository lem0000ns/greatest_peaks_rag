Ballycastle
Ballycastle is a town on the north coast of Northern Ireland, and home of the Ballycastle Bats, who are members of the British and Irish Quidditch League (QA7).
Location: County Antrim, Northern Ireland
Ballycastle (Irish Gaelic Baile an Chaistil, "town of the castle") is located in County Antrim, Northern Ireland. It is part of the United Kingdom. The game of Quidditch is mentioned on its Wikipedia page.
