					Granville Jorkins
			
Gender Male Dates fl. 1993
First Introduced DP2: Daily Prophet Issue 2

The birth of his sister Griselda Harmonia Jorkins was announced in the Daily Prophet (DP2)
The birth of sister Griselda to his parents Primrose and Albert Jorkins occurred on December 6, 1998. He already has a brother Grimwold (DP2)
While the date printed on DP2 is 8 February 1999, the timeframe for these events is 1992-1993.
