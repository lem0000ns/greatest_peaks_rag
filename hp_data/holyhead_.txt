Holyhead
Holyhead is a port city located on an island at the northwest tip of Wales, and is the home of the Holyhead Harpies, an eight-hundred year old Quidditch team and a famous member of the British and Irish Quidditch League (QA7).
Holyhead (Welsh: Caergybi) is located on Holy Island, on the west side of the larger Island of Anglesey (Welsh: Ynys Môn) (Holyhead Town Council). It is the main UK seaport for ferries to Dublin in Ireland (Wikipedia).
