-- Ginny Weasley's report for the Daily Prophet, 13 April 2014 (Pm)
Elodie Dembélé was one of the Chasers on the Ivory Coast National Team at the Quidditch World Cup tournament in 2014 (Pm:Quidditch).
Although Dembélé scored most of Ivory Coast’s goals, her team lost their initial match against the Norwegian National Team by a score of 340 points to 100 (Pm:Quidditch).
Dembélé was eighteen years old as of 13 April 2014.
Elodie is the French form of the Visigothic name Alodia, derived from alja "other, foreign" and aud "riches, wealth" (Behind the Name).
Dembélé is a West African surname (Wikipedia). There is an Ivorian television news presenter called Habiba Dembélé.
