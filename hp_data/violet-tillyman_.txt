Violet Tillyman


					Violet Tillyman
			
Gender Female Dates fl. 1855
First Introduced Pm: Pottermore

Violet Tillyman was a witch who was famous for apparently vanishing in the Floo Network after a row with her husband Albert. Her disappearance caused a national scare in which people stopped using the Floo Network for fear that they, too, would vanish. In reality, she had simply ended up in the wrong grate and fallen in love with Myron Otherhaus, the man she met there. She never returned to her husband, only revealing what had happened after the his death 20 years later (Pm).
Spouse: Albert Tillyman, a suspicious bully. She never returned to him after she met Myron Otherhaus.
Children: Violet had seven children with Myron.
