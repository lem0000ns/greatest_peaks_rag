Ethelred the Ever-Ready


					Ethelred the Ever-Ready
			
Gender Male Dates fl. Middle Ages
Species / Race Wizard Distinguishing Features Cursing and yelling at others
First Introduced FW: The Famous Wizard Cards

Ethelred the Ever-Ready was a medieval wizard famous for taking offense at nothing and cursing innocent bystanders. He died in jail (FW).
Ever-ready to start a fight.
Old Saxon athal meaning "noble", and ræd meaning "counsel" or "advice".
Name is probably a pun on the English king Æthelred the Unready.
