Justus Pilliwickle was a former head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement (FW).
In the Bible, Justus is another name for Barsabbas, one of the two disciples considered to replace Judas as one of the twelve apostles. Lots were drawn to decide, and Justus lost out to Matthias (Acts 1:21–26).
A homophone to "justice" and the Latin word for "just" (in the latter case, not a homophone).
