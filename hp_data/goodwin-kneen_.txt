					Goodwin Kneen
			
Gender Male Species / Race Wizard
First Introduced QA3: The Game From Queerditch Marsh

Goodwin Kneen was a twelfth century wizard who wrote about a Yorkshire Quidditch match to his cousin in Norway (QA3, QA7).
Goodwin Kneen's letter to his cousin Olaf is now famous because it is an early record of Quidditch.
