Madame Bonhabille


					Madame Bonhabille
			
Gender Female Species / Race wizard
Profession hatmaker
First Introduced Pm: Pottermore

Madame Bonhabille was a nineteenth-century hatmaker in Paris who was commissioned by Eupraxia Mole to make Peeves a new hat in 1876.
"Bon" is French for "good," and "habille" is French for "dressy" or "dressed" - so Madame Bonhabille is literally "well-dressed."
