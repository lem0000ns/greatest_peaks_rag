					Marlow
			
Species / Race Bowtruckle
First Introduced WFT: Fantastic Beast and Where to Find Them (film)

Marlow was a Bowtruckle in Newt Scamander’s case (WFT).
Pickett was afraid that the other Bowtruckles, including Marlow, would be mean to him. Marlow and his fellow Bowtruckles besides Pickett allegedly felt that Newt showed favoritism towards Pickett (WFT).
Part of a clan, or "branch", or Bowtruckles in a tree in Newt Scamander's case (WFT).
