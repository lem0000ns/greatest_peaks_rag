Caerphilly (Caerffili)
Caerphilly has been the home of the Caerphilly Catapults, a Quidditch team in the British and Irish Quidditch League since 1402 (QA8). The town located in South Wales.
Located 12 km north of the Welsh capital Caerdydd (Cardiff) in South Wales, it was the location of a 1st century Roman fort. Although it has a professional Quidditch team, its Wikipedia page does not list any wizarding sports or any other wizarding activity (Wikipedia).
