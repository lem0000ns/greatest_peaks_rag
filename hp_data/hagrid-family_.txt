					Hagrid family
			
Species / Race Wizard/Giant

A family in the Wizarding world with mixed human and giant members. Known members:
Rubeus Hagrid
Hagrid Sr.
Fridwulfa
Grawp
Hagrid Sr. married Fridwulfa, a giantess. They had a son who they named Rubeus Hagrid. Fridwulfa left her husband and young son and returned to the giants where she bore another son by another giant. This child was named Grawp.
