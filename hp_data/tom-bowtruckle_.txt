					Tom (Bowtruckle)
			
Gender Male Species / Race Bowtruckle
First Introduced WFT: Fantastic Beast and Where to Find Them (film)

Tom was a Bowtruckle in Newt Scamander’s case (WFT).
Pickett was afraid that the other Bowtruckles, including Tom, would be mean to him. Tom and his fellow Bowtruckles besides Pickett allegedly felt that Newt showed favoritism towards Pickett (WFT).
Part of a clan, or "branch", or Bowtruckles in a tree in Newt Scamander's case (WFT).
