Ulick Gamp was the head of the Wizengamot before he became the first Minister for Magic, in office from 1701 to 1718. He founded the Department of Magical Law Enforcement (Pm).
A portrait of Ulick Gamp hangs in the Prime Minister's office at 10 Downing Street (HBP1).
