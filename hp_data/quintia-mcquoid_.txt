					Quintia McQuoid
			
Gender Female Dates d. 1407
Death 1407
Species / Race Witch
Profession Potioneer
First Introduced BoP: Book of Potions (game)

"... Quintia McQuoid, that most memorable of potioneers, whose love affair with potions sadly came to an end with her untimely death from drinking an imperfectly-brewed cure for ague."
-- Announcer on Book of Potions (BoP)
Quintia McQuoid was a potioneer who died 1407 from consuming an improperly brewed Cure for Ague Potion. The Wizarding Schools Potions Championship is held in her honor (BoP).
