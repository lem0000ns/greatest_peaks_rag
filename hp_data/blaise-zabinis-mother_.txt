					Blaise Zabini’s mother
			
Gender Female Species / Race Witch Distinguishing Features Famous for her beauty -- and the short life spans of her many husbands
First Introduced HBP7: The Slug Club

Blaise Zabini’s mother was a “famously beautiful witch” who “married seven times.” Each of her wealthy husbands died a mysterious death, leaving her a lot of gold (HBP7). She is the mother of Blaise Zabini, a Slytherin in Harry’s year.
She married seven times (HBP7). Seven recurs as a highly significant number throughout the series. -BB
