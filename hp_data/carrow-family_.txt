					Carrow family
			

A Wizarding Pure-blood family, listed in the ‘Pure-Blood Directory’ (c. 1930) as one of the ‘Sacred Twenty-Eight’ families (Pm).
Notable members:
Alecto Carrow
Amycus Carrow
The Carrow siblings were Death Eaters who worked at Hogwarts under Snape's tenure as headmaster (DH29).
