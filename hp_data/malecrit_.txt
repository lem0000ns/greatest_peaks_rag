Gender Male Species / Race Wizard
Profession Playwright
First Introduced QA8: The Spread of Quidditch Worldwide

Malécrit was a wizard playwright. He wrote Hélas, Je me suis Transfiguré mes Pieds (“Alas, I have Transfigured my Feet“) (QA8).
"malécrit" = French for "badly written" (thanks to Anne-Claire Soussan).
