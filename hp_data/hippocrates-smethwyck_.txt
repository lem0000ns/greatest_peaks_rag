					Hippocrates Smethwyck
			
Gender Male Species / Race Wizard
Profession Healer-in-Charge
First Introduced OP22: St Mungo’s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries

Hippocrates Smethwyck was Healer-in-Charge for the “Dangerous” Dai Llewellyn Ward for Serious Bites at St. Mungo’s Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries (OP22).
Augustus Pye was a Trainee Healer under Smethwyck's supervision (OP22).
Hippocrates was an ancient Greek physician who is often considered to be the father of modern medicine (source: Wikipedia).
