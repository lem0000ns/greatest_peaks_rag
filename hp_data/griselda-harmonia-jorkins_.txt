					Griselda Harmonia Jorkins
			
Gender Female Dates fl. 1993
First Introduced DP2: Daily Prophet Issue 2

The birth of Griselda Harmonia Jorkins was announced in the Daily Prophet (DP2)
 
Primrose and Albert Jorkins, already the parents of Grimwold and Granville, had their third child, Griselda, on December 6, 1998 (DP2)
While the date printed on DP2 is 8 February 1999, the timeframe for these events is 1992-1993.
