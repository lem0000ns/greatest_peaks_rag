							Braking charm
					
Magic Type
				
Charms Magical effect Transportation 


The Braking Charm allows a wizarding broom to stop effectively.
First introduced in 1929 for the Comet series of racing brooms, the Horton-Keitch Braking Charm was patented by Randolph Keitch and Basil Horton (QA9).
A Firebolt has an "unbreakable Braking Charm" on it (PA4).
