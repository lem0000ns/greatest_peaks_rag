					Leticia Somnolens
			
Gender Female Species / Race Hag
First Introduced FW: The Famous Wizard Cards

Leticia Somnolens was a Hag who used the Draught of Living Death to put a princess to sleep (FW).
Leticia: Variation of "Letitia," which comes from a Latin name meaning "joy" or "happiness" (source: Behind the Name).
Somnolens: "Somnolence" is a medical term for drowsiness (source: Wikipedia). "Somnus" is Latin for "sleep" (source: Wiktionary).
