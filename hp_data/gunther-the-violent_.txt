Günther the Violent


					Günther the Violent
			
Gender Male Species / Race Wizard
First Introduced QA2: Ancient Broom Games

Günther the Violent was a Medieval German Stichstock player and a subject in the 1105 painting Günther der Gewalttätig ist der Gewinner (“Günther the Violent is the Winner”), which is the first recording of Stichstock (QA2).
