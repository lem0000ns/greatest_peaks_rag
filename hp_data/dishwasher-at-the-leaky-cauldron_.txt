					Dishwasher at the Leaky Cauldron
			
Gender Male Species / Race Wizard
Profession Dishwasher
First Introduced GF9: The Dark Mark

Young wizard who tried to impress Veela in the forest near the campground of the Quidditch World Cup by telling them that he was a dragon killer for the Committee for the Disposal of Dangerous Creatures. He was actually a dishwasher at the Leaky Cauldron. (GF9).
