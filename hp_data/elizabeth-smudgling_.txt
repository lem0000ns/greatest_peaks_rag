					Elizabeth Smudgling
			
Gender Female Dates fl. 1379
Species / Race Witch
First Introduced BoS: Book of Spells

A duelling competition held on Dartmoor in 1379 was won by the English witch Elizabeth Smudgling, who cleverly used a simple Disarming Charm against her wizard opponent in the final. For this achievement, she became the “Supreme Dueller”. (BoS)
