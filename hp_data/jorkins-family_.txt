					Jorkins family
			
Dates fl. 1990s
First Introduced DP2: Daily Prophet Issue 2

The Jorkins are a wizarding family.
Bertha Jorkins (GF1, GF5, GF7, GF19, GF24) – it may be that Bertha is related to the Jorkins family mentioned in the Daily Prophet birth announcement.
Primrose Jorkins – mother of new baby Griselda (DP2).
Albert Jorkins – father of new baby Griselda (DP2).
Granville Jorkins – brother of new baby Griselda (DP2).
Grimwold Jorkins – brother of new baby Griselda (DP2).
Griselda Harmonia Jorkins – the announcement of a new female addition to the family appeared in the “Births” section of the Daily Prophet (DP2).
Although the date printed on the second Daily Prophet Newsletter (DP2) is 8 February 1999, the timeframe for this event is 1992-1993.
Writing by J K Rowling on Pottermore: The Daily Prophet
